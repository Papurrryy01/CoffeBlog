3 Delicius Coffee Recipes

1) Classic Iced Latte:

- Brew a strong espresso, pour it over ice, and top with cold milk. Smooth, refreshing, and perfect for hot days.

2) Mocha Delight:

- Combine espresso with hot cocoa and steamed milk, then finish with whipped cream. A chocolatey twist on your daily cup.

3) Vanilla Cold Brew:

- Steep coarse coffee grounds in cold water overnight, strain, and add a splash of vanilla syrup. Sweet, smooth, and naturally less acidic.
